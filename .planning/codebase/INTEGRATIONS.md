# Integrations: TresArt

## Internal APIs
- **Products API**: `/api/products` (GET) - Fetches list of hand-painted products.

## External Services
- **Unsplash**: Used for placeholder product images.
- **Google Fonts**: Potential usage for typography (Playfair Display, etc.)

## Assets
- **Images**: Located in `client/public` and external URLs.
- **Audio**: Located in `client/public/sounds`.
