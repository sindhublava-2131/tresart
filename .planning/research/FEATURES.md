# Features: TresArt (Luxury Gallery)

## Table Stakes (Must-Have)
- **Responsive Homepage**: Fluid layout that looks stunning on mobile and desktop.
- **Minimal Branding**: Large, elegant typography with the "Feel the Artistry" tagline.
- **Product Gallery**: High-quality imagery of hand-painted tote bags.
- **Product Details**: Basic info (price, description) fetched from the backend.
- **Navigation**: Simple, transparent navbar that lets the art be the hero.

## Luxury Differentiators (Wow Factor)
- **Micro-Animations**: Subtle "reveal" animations as users scroll or hover over products.
- **Artisan Process**: A dedicated section explaining the "hand-painted" nature of the brand.
- **Fluid Layout**: Grid layouts that avoid a "boxy" tech feel, using generous whitespace.

## Out of Scope (For Now)
- Authentication (Login/Signup)
- Shopping Cart & Checkout
- Artist Dashboard
